Overview

Purpose

"To Easy Math" is a site that contains several calculators and math instructions. This site will cover both simple and complex math. This is intended to help people go through math problems and check them through a calculator. Additionally it could be used as a teacher resource to help students with there math.

Audience

This site is made for those who need to check or solve various forms of math. The people who will generally use this are teachers or students. Lots of math problems require references to complete correctly which will be provided on this site. Additional math sources will be put in this site.
